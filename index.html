FAQ
Do I need to have the title?

Having the title makes things faster, but it's not always required. We handle title research and surety bond processes for abandoned or title-less vessels. Tell us your situation on the call and we'll advise.

What if the boat is sunken or can't be trailered?

That's a specialty of ours. We have the equipment for in-water extractions, grounded vessels, and difficult-access locations. It affects the quote, not whether we can help.

Do you work with insurance companies?

Yes. For storm damage claims we coordinate directly with adjusters, provide documentation, and manage the timeline around claim approvals.

How fast can you actually show up?

Most jobs are scheduled within 3–7 days of accepting the quote. Storm emergencies and marina-pressure situations move faster. We'll give you a real date on the call.

What happens to my boat after removal?

Sellable vessels are resold or donated. Non-salvageable boats are dismantled and disposed of through licensed facilities. We handle title surrender and environmental compliance.
